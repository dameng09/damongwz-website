About Dameng New Materials

关于大梦新材料

Dameng New Materials Co., Ltd. is a professional company specializing in silicone oil raw materials. We are committed to providing high-quality, cost-effective silicone products to global customers.

Our product range includes Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS), Cosmetic Grade Silicone Oil, Food Grade Silicone Oil, Mold Release Silicone Oil, Antifoam Silicone Oil, and more. They are widely used in cosmetics, personal care, coatings, textiles, plastics, rubber, agriculture, and electronics industries.

We adhere to the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Priority." Through long-term partnerships with source factories, we ensure stable product quality and reliable supply. Whether standard products or custom formulations, we provide professional solutions and attentive service.
